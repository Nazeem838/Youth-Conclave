MGMU Youth Conclave

A Movement to Channelize Youth

17th & 18th February 2023

MGM University, Aurangabad, M.S

About The event

Mahatma Gandhi Mission (MGM) University, Aurangabad is proud to host the MGMU Youth Conclave 2023
with a vision to provide a platform to the Youth, to address the issues and challenges faced by society,
interact with eminent speakers to explore solutions and create a strong social impact.

This is a unique opportunity to participate in the first-of-its-kind Youth Movement in the Marathwada region.

    • When

    Friday - Saturday
    17th & 18th February, 2023

    • Location

    Rukmini Auditorium, MGM Campus,
    N-6, CIDCO, Aurangabad

    MOTIVE

    Every year atleast one issue to be resolved and implemented in the
    region post the conclave by students of MGM UNIVERSITY

    INSIGHTS OF THE EVENT

    Expert Interaction

    Participants will attend Panel discussions featuring 7+ eminent speakers who will share their insights on the theme. These sessions will be followed by Q&A rounds, where the participants will have a chance to interact with the speakers and get their voice heard.

    Solution Presentation

    The participants will present their solutions infront of the Jury in the form of a PPT presentation. The best solutions will be awarded.

    Approach to resolve the issue

    Practically Feasible Solution

    Conclave Theme 2023

    Youth Addictions

    Social Media & Cyberbullying
